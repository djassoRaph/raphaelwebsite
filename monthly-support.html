Common questions

What's the difference between paying per incident and a monthly plan?

Break-fix means you pay each time something goes wrong ; usually €100–€200 per callout, and whoever shows up doesn't know your setup. A monthly plan means one fixed cost, one engineer who already knows your systems, and problems caught before they become emergencies. See also: a full breakdown of outsourced IT options.

Is there a minimum commitment?

No long-term contract required. Plans are month-to-month ; if it doesn't work for you, you stop. No cancellation fee, no notice period.

Do you work with companies outside of France?

Yes. CET-based but with clients across the US, Caribbean, and Asia. Timezone differences are manageable for most small business IT needs.
