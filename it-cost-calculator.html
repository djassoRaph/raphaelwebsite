Why These Numbers?

Most small businesses underestimate what IT problems actually cost. The calculator above looks at three things: how often problems happen, how much they cost when they do, and what prevention would save you over a year.

Scenario Typical annual cost
Break-fix: 4 call-outs per year at €200 each €800 — plus your time
One serious incident (data loss, hack) €2,000–€10,000+
Monthly IT support — Essential plan €3,000/year, fixed
Monthly IT support — Starter plan €900/year, fixed

The break-fix model feels cheaper until the day it isn't. Monthly support is a predictable line in the budget; not a surprise invoice after a crisis.
